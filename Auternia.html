Auternia

”The Vibrant Autumn planet: a land rich in art, culture, and history.
Auternia holds centuries of tales and legends.
Never equilibrating, the karmic balance shifts on an astronomical scale. ”

Endeavours to quell this realm have always been ill fated regardless of righteous or malicious intent. Igniting the burning soul of this world is Chi energy. Granting both the soil on which to stand and the skies to explore, without it, nothing could be. Blossoming the totality of all in existence, Chi is the greatest force this universe knows.

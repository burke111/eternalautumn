About Us


The Eternal Autumn Project is a multimedia fantasy venture collecting influence from Eastern media, science fiction, fantasy, and adventure. With role models such as Studio Ghibli, Michael Dante DiMartino and Bryan Konietzko (creators of Avatar: The Last Airbender and The Legend of Korra), and Studio Chizu, our aim is to produce titles to capture and honour the art.

  

   

Rather than a singular creation, the Eternal Autumn Project is a long term, expansive universe with stories to be told through many different mediums. Games, novels, graphic novels, animations, songs, and more, are all within our sights.
